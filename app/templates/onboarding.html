Manuscript transcribes, separates speakers, and lets you annotate long-form audio — built for researchers, journalists, and anyone who reads closely. Three quick steps and you're set.

{# STEP 1 — Hugging Face token (required + live-verified) #}
STEP 02 / 04 · ACCESS

Connect Hugging Face.
Optional.

Speaker diarization works out of the box — the pyannote model is bundled with Manuscript. Adding a free Hugging Face token is optional; it only lets you fetch model updates later. Paste one to verify and save it, or just continue.

read access
Stored in your operating system's secure keyring — never written to disk in plaintext, never uploaded.
How to get your token
  1. Open huggingface.co/settings/tokens and sign in (or create a free account).
  2. Click New token, give it a name, and choose the Read role.
  3. Accept the user conditions on {{ diarize_model }}.
  4. Copy the token (starts with hf_) and paste it above.
